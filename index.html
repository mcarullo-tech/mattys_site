About Me

My name is Matthew and I am a fucking idiot from fucking nowhere. Man it feels so weird to introduce myself without the obligation to state my profession. I am so used to giving my rehearsed speech of "Hi, my name is Matthew and I'm a mechanical engineer from..." It's weird that this has sort of become my identity when in reality I don't associate myself with it at all. In fact, I don't like it very much at all, yet is has become such a huge part of who I am. I guess that's something to think about.

In the jovial spirit of identity crisis, I have created this site to help me pivot to another career which I have no interest in: software development! You know what they say, when one type of engineering isn't working out for you, the obvious path forward is to sink a bunch of time into another!

But in all seriousness, I am hoping this can be a sort of creative outlet for me. I have been feeling the desire to create something, but it has been difficult to figure out what. I feel like a website/blog would be an interesting idea because it is a sort of intersection between what I want to do (be creative) and what I feel like I should do (Learn to code).

In any case, hope you enjoy my site!
